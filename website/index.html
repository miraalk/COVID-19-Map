About this map

This map displays confirmed cases, Candians who are potential carriers of the virus, as well highlight regions where there are many elderly and immunocompromised individuals. The purpose of this map is to inform government agencies and healthcare systems of where there is an influx of potential cases to arise so that resources can be utilized in the most efficient manner. Additionally, through highlighting regions where there is a dense demographic of vulnerable individuals, we can inform agencies to have the resources to protect these individuals.

The "confirmed" layer shows flags where individuals who have tested positive for COVID-19 are mapped. Through the submission of FLATTEN, the vulnerable populations as well as potential carriers are mapped.

Data Sources:

Potential Cases and High-risk Populations: flatten.ca
Confirmed Cases: COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group. Epidemiological Data from the COVID-19 Outbreak in Canada.
